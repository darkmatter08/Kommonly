How It Works


List your Event on Kommonly It's as easy as 1,2,3.

Simply create your event and tell us what it's all about and what you're looking for. This is your chance to show off how awesome your event will be and how it will reach tens (or thousands) of attendees!

Connect with potential sponsors with a simple search.

Once your event is created, you can search for companies that would love to sponsor your event in terms of money, swag, venue, or whatever you need!

Everyone wins.

By creating these connections, Kommonly helps both companies and event organizers in a mutually benefecial way. For companies, we give you access to the best events to show off your brand, and we give you event organizers the resources to make sure your event stands out.
